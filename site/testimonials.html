Before & After

Three leaders, three different stuck states, three different outcomes.

The Situation

VP Engineering at a Series B tech scale-up. Delivering results but going blank in board presentations. Described it as "my brain just stops." Had been operating with unrecognised cognitive depletion for months.

The Work

LeadStrong 12-week programme. CliftonStrengths and amygdala-PFC diagnostic. Identified overactive threat response in high-stakes evaluation contexts. Brain retraining protocols for verbal fluency under pressure. Stress tolerance progressively increased across the programme.

The Outcome

Back to making clean decisions within six weeks. Board presentations no longer a source of dread. Leading them rather than surviving them. "I was back to making clean decisions within six weeks."

VP Engineering · Tech scale-up · LeadStrong
The Situation

Founder and CEO of a Series B SaaS company. Functional but not sharp. Decision-making felt laboured, strategic clarity gone. Had been carrying the business through a difficult growth period and hadn't stopped to reset.

The Work

2-Day Cognitive Reset at Sopwell House. Somatic movement sessions to downregulate the amygdala. Private coaching to restore strategic perspective. Structured spa restoration to allow neuroplasticity to embed the shift. Followed by 7-day and 30-day integration sessions.

The Outcome

Left the retreat thinking more clearly than he had in a year. Described the experience as "rigorous, evidence-based work" not a wellness break. Strategic clarity returned within the programme. Decision quality measurably improved in the following quarter.
