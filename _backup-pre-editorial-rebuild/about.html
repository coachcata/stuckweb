About

I Get Leaders
Unstuck

Throughout my professional career, I've always found myself coaching people — no matter my job title. Holding space for leaders. Helping them see what they couldn't see alone. Guiding them from where they are to where they need to be.

My Story

I Help Leaders Identify Where They're Stuck

That's when I discovered what I do differently: I help leaders identify where they're stuck, create a plan to get unstuck, move forward sustainably — and build the stress tolerance to handle what's coming next.

I saw the same pattern repeatedly: brilliant, capable leaders operating at pace — holding pressure, carrying emotional weight, and rarely giving themselves space to think.

They didn't need another training day or productivity hack. They needed structured brain reset and stress tolerance training.

Why I Built This Practice

A Practice Rooted in Evidence, Not Fluff

What I Use

Psychometrics

CliftonStrengths and FITT16 — diagnostic precision that reveals how you're wired to lead.

Brain Retraining Protocols

Neuroscience-backed practices to retrain the amygdala-PFC patterns keeping you stuck.

Stress Tolerance Training

Progressive capacity building so your brain stays online when stakes rise.

Strategic Frameworks

Playing to Win and Essentialism — rigorous approaches to sustainable performance.

What I Don't Use

❌ Motivational slogans or vague wellness practices

❌ Therapy or mental health treatment

❌ Generic corporate coaching playbooks

❌ Dependency models that keep you coming back indefinitely

This is performance coaching grounded in neuroscience.

Why I'm Different

Built Outside the System

I'm a young, Black, non-corporate coach. I built my practice outside traditional coaching because burnt-out leaders don't trust the systems that exhausted them. And stuck leaders don't trust coaches who've never been stuck themselves.

I bring frameworks, not fluff. Psychology, not platitudes. Strategic rigour, not wellness cliches.
