02 · The Reality

Six things that keep founders stuck.

This isn't failure. It's what happens when you build something from scratch and the business outgrows the way you've been running it.

i.

Being owned by your business

You built it. Now it runs you. You can't step back without feeling it'll fall apart.

ii.

Wearing too many hats

CEO, CFO, head of sales, customer support. You know it isn't scalable, but you can't see the path out.

iii.

Strategic thinking gone narrow

Stuck in the weeds. The big-picture thinking that got you here? You can't access it any more.

iv.

Decisions that deplete you

Every decision drains you. You're making hundreds a week. And you can't tell which ones actually matter.

v.

Team dynamics you can't fix

You hired people to help. But you're still the bottleneck. And you don't know why.

vi.

Stress at your ceiling

Your brain has learned to handle a certain level. But scaling requires more. And you're already at your threshold.

03 · The Method

Pause. Reset. Stay unstuck.

The same architecture as Lead Strong, but applied iteratively as the business grows. One method. Three movements. Repeated for each new stuck state at each new stage.

PauseFirst month Diagnose. CliftonStrengths reveals how you're wired. FITT16 reveals your purpose. Amygdala-PFC assessment maps when your brain gets stuck and your current stress tolerance threshold.
ResetOngoing Retrain. Each session tackles your current stuck state — cognitive reset, strategic clarity, team challenges, scaling decisions. Brain retraining protocols matched to your pattern. Structured work, not passive support.
GoLong-term Stay unstuck. Build systems so you don't get stuck in the same places again. As the business grows, we work on the new stuck areas of the next stage and progressively increase your stress capacity.

05 · Why Once Isn't Enough

Different growth stages create different stuck states.

The bottleneck at £100K isn't the bottleneck at £1M. BuildStrong gets you unstuck at each stage and builds the stress capacity the next one demands.

i. £0 – 100K

Stuck doing everything

Handling uncertainty and scarcity. Every task falls on you. You can't tell what to prioritise.

ii. £100K – 500K

Stuck hiring the wrong people

Handling team conflict and delegation. You hired help — but you're still doing it all yourself.

iii. £500K – 1M

Stuck in operational chaos

Handling operational complexity. Systems breaking. You're firefighting instead of building.

iv. £1M+

Stuck being the bottleneck

Handling board pressure and scale-up demands. Everything still flows through you.

I help you get unstuck at each stage and build the stress capacity the next stage requires.
