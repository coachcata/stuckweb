The Reality

Founders Get Stuck. Repeatedly.

Being owned by your business
You built it. Now it runs you. You can't step back without feeling like it'll fall apart.
Wearing too many hats
You're the CEO, CFO, head of sales, and customer support. You know this isn't scalable, but you can't see the path out.
Strategic thinking that's gone narrow
You're stuck in the weeds. The big-picture thinking that got you here? You can't access it.
Decision-making that depletes you
Every decision drains you. You're making hundreds per week. And you can't tell which ones actually matter.
Team dynamics you can't fix
You hired people to help. But you're still the bottleneck. And you don't know why.
Stress levels you can't sustain
Your brain has learned to handle a certain level of stress. But scaling requires handling more. And you're already at your threshold.

This isn't failure. This is what happens when you build something from scratch. Different stuck states require different solutions. BuildStrong gives you flexible support to get unstuck — whatever the current stuck area is — while building your stress tolerance for the next stage.

The Difference

BuildStrong vs LeadStrong

LeadStrong

Designed for senior leaders in established organisations — VPs and C-suite executives navigating corporate complexity, board dynamics, and large team leadership. Fixed 12-week programme.

BuildStrong

Built for founders and small business owners facing different challenges: wearing multiple hats, building from scratch, managing cash flow pressure, hiring your first team, and scaling without losing what made you successful. Flexible ongoing support.
